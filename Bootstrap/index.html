    Why Innovators Academy works

    Personalized learning

    Students practice at their own pace, first filling in gaps in their understanding and then accelerating their learning.

    Trusted content

    Created by experts, Innovators Academy’s library of trusted, standards-aligned practice and lessons covers math K-12 through early college, grammar, science, history, SAT®, and more. It’s all free for learners and teachers.

    Tools to empower teachersg

    With Innovators Academy, teachers can identify gaps in their students’ understanding, tailor instruction, and meet the needs of every student.
